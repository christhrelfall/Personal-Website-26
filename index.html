— What I do

I specialise in design system strategy — from creation through to implementation across multiple products — while actively adopting and integrating AI tools to enhance both design and business workflows. My work spans the full end-to-end UX process, from early discovery and user research through to delivery, grounding every decision in usability testing and data. I bring strong visual and interaction design across web, mobile, and enterprise applications, and work closely with product, development, and business stakeholders throughout, taking ownership of projects and delivering within agile environments. With a technical background in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I bridge the gap between design and code — and I'm always looking to improve the processes, tools, and standards that help teams do their best work.
